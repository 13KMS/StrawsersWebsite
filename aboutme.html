Hello, there! ⤷ ゛🦈ˎˊ˗

My name is Kalista, you can just call me Kali if you like.

I love reading, writing, and drawing. I have always been in love with the arts and hope to spread that creativity and imagination to others.

I've taken classes of Graphic Design, Photography, Ceramics, 3D Design, and now Web Design which I'm hoping to master as well. I want to show my work and how I can bring a new set of ideas and a fresh mind.

My parents and friends are my heros and close family. I have a female dog who's a Pitbull, her name is Luna; she's just so sweet and cute! I love to play video games like "The Last Of Us", "Fallout 4", "Ender Lilies", and "Stray".

Indie, Alternative, and Rock are my favorite music genres but I also love to play Indie games as they have some of the best works. I wish to be a Video Game Designer when I grow up.



I plan on getting into Penn. State or Cal. Arts for college/university, after I graduate from highschool and get my general studies done at Mira Coasta Community College.

I like cozy, inspiring spaces where I can create a room filled with art supplies, some plants, and my favorite music playing in the background. While I enjoy collaborating with others creatively, and working with a team that blends art, storytelling, and tech.

I have a close-knit group of friends who are also into creative things, and love sharing ideas and collaborating with me. I have a ritual for starting new projects, like looking at references, putting on certain music, and doodling to warm up my brain.

I get really into world-building when I write about backstories, culture, and settings in detail. Cause of this I sometimes lose track of time when I'm deep in my art or gaming.
